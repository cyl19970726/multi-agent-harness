# Company OS V2 page matrix

## P0 mother pages

| Page | State | Product question | Primary template | Pressure state | Explicit exclusions |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Company Home | morning operating review | What changed, what needs Human/Lead attention, and which Milestones are at risk? | editorial operating brief | one pending financial approval and one at-risk Milestone | no generic KPI card wall, no raw runtime logs, no Project object |
| Docs Workspace | company knowledge home | Where does company knowledge live, and how do Documents, Modules, Milestones, and records connect? | nested knowledge workspace | proposed Trademark Management module | no file-manager grid, no detached database app, no copied finance truth |
| Organization | lead-first company | Who governs the company, who reports to the Lead, and where is capability missing? | organization canvas | proposed second-level Trademark Agent | no runtime roster, no AgentTeamRun members, no deep hierarchy by default |
| Lead Agent Workspace | coordinating direct reports | What are direct Agents doing, what is blocked, and what should the Lead do next? | collaboration workspace | Finance Agent waiting on Human decision; capability proposal | no provider transcript, no persisted thinking, no TeamRun console semantics |
| Business Module | trademark operations | How does one recurring business function combine knowledge, roles, Milestones, work, approvals, and finance? | domain operating page | filing blocked by Human approval | no orphan dashboard, no duplicated records, no Project layer |
| Work | milestone operating view | Which company outcomes and WorkItems remain, who owns them, and what is blocked? | milestone roadmap plus dense work ledger | one blocked development item and one waiting-for-approval legal item | no Task Graph, no Project hierarchy, no Wave-as-Milestone |

## V2-derived page families

After the P0 mother pages are reviewed, V2 expands to:

- Document Focus and structured View;
- second-level Standing Agent Workspace and Human Member Focus;
- Milestone Focus and typed WorkItem Focus variants: development, procurement,
  legal, content, governance, and Human Action;
- Approval Focus and Finance;
- Mission/Wave, Agent Team Run, Member Run, and Workflow Run;
- Organization/BusinessModule governance proposals;
- responsive tablet and mobile states for every shell-sensitive P0 page.

## Shared scenario

The first design series uses one company context:

- Human Owner: Brand Owner;
- Lead Agent: Company Lead;
- direct reports: Document Architecture Agent, Finance Agent, Content Strategy
  Agent, and a proposed Trademark Agent;
- BusinessModule: Trademark Management;
- Milestone: Application package ready;
- WorkItems: confirm classes, run similarity research, prepare application,
  approve the CNY 3,000 filing commitment;
- decision pressure: Brand Owner must approve the commitment before filing;
- one-time execution: similarity research may start a Mission with Waves and a
  temporary Agent Team, but those objects remain drill-ins.
