# V2.2 shared generation contract

Use case: `ui-mockup`

Asset type: realistic desktop product UI mockup, 1536 × 1024.

Primary request: revise the supplied Company OS V2 page into an
**art-directed workbench**. Preserve its product workflow, page regions,
information density, and practical desktop proportions. Make it feel authored,
recognisable, and inviting through a restrained visual asset layer.

Visual language:

- warm ivory canvas and pale stone shell;
- graphite text, coral-red primary accent, sage operational state, amber
  attention state, muted blue relationship state;
- crisp modern sans-serif interface typography with occasional editorial
  display emphasis;
- hairline dividers, quiet tinted shadows, tactile paper/ceramic materiality;
- canonical portrait-style Agent avatars with clear role identity;
- one dominant page-specific art anchor and at most two supporting motifs;
- elegant line-work, engraved diagrams, small editorial emblems, and restrained
  spatial depth;
- dense operational rows stay calm, aligned, and readable.

Constraints:

- preserve the supplied page's primary regions and workflow;
- no generic SaaS card wall, no glassmorphism, no purple AI gradient, no neon,
  no oversized hero art, no marketing landing-page composition;
- no Task Graph, Project object, GoalPhase, or Wave-as-Milestone;
- no invented KPI charts, fake payments, extra approvals, or new hierarchy;
- do not persist or expose chain-of-thought;
- no watermark;
- generated text may be abbreviated, but all primary labels must remain legible
  and semantically correct.
