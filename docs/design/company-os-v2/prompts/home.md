# Company Home · V2 expected prompt

Use case: ui-mockup. Asset: shippable desktop web-app product mockup, 1536×1024.

Create a premium AI-native Company OS morning operating brief. The user asks:
what changed, what needs Human or Lead attention, and which Milestones are at
risk? Use a warm ivory canvas, milk-white editorial surfaces, graphite type,
restrained coral focus, sage and amber semantics, hairline borders, controlled
10–16 px radii, subtle material depth, excellent typography. No purple, no
gradient, no generic KPI card grid, no BI wall, no illustrations.

Shared shell: 240 px warm stone sidebar with Home active, Docs, Organization,
Work, Approvals, Finance; separate Execution group for Missions, Workflows,
Agent Teams. Quiet 56 px top bar with search and Human avatar. Main page feels
like a crafted editorial briefing, not a dashboard.

Main reading order: small date and greeting “Good morning”; title “Company
briefing”; a refined horizontal attention ribbon with “1 decision”, “1 at-risk
milestone”, and “4 active agents”. Below, use a dominant two-column composition.
Left broad column: “Needs your decision” as an elegant highlighted narrative
panel for “Approve CNY 3,000 trademark filing commitment”, showing Brand Owner
as required Human approver, Trademark Agent as requester, evidence count, and a
coral “Review decision” action. Then “Milestones” as three sophisticated rows
with fine progress lines: “Application package ready” at risk, “July content
calendar” on track, “Document architecture baseline” achieved. Show remaining
WorkItems and owners, not charts. Right narrower column: “Lead Agent brief” with
a concise natural-language summary, direct report avatars, one blocker, and
“Open Lead workspace”; below, “Recent company changes” as a clean source-labelled
timeline linking Docs, Work, Finance. Use real short readable English labels.

Constraints: no Project object, no Task Graph, no raw runtime logs, no provider
telemetry, no persisted thinking, no invented payment, no floating modal, no
watermark, no browser chrome.
