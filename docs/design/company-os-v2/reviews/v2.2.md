## Result

- V2 layout and functional density remain intact across all six P0 pages.
- the series now has a recognisable visual identity: warm paper material,
  graphite typography, coral pressure/action, sage operating truth, engraved
  relationship line-work, canonical actor portraits, and role-specific emblems;
- each page uses art differently according to its product question rather than
  applying one decorative template everywhere;
- dense ledgers, document content, authority text, and financial truth remain
  quieter than the page's dominant visual anchor;
- Work now shows the financial commitment acceptance criterion as pending;
- Trademark Management is shown as Active because the page contains an active
  application.

## Generated-image deviations

The following are image-generation artifacts, not design decisions:

- Organization's generated page title reads `Company OS`; the product title is
  `Organization`;
- generated people names, dates, portrait mappings, small icons, and secondary
  copy are not canonical;
- generated small icon shapes demonstrate weight and placement only. Production
  icons come from one scalable vector family;
- a generated portrait never changes ActorRef identity or actor type.

## Approval question

Human review should decide whether V2.2 has the right degree of beauty and
visual character for a serious daily workbench. Approval covers hierarchy,
materiality, asset density, visual rhythm, and page-specific art direction. It
does not approve generated copy or data.
